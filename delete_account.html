Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Learn how to permanently delete your Zayda account.

Important notice: Deleting your account is a permanent action. Your profile details, active sessions, saved addresses, favorites list, and shopping cart will be deleted immediately. Transaction records (e.g. invoice logs of past orders) will be securely archived and anonymized to comply with legal and financial reporting regulations.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Zayda app on your mobile device.
  2. Go to Profile settings in the navigation menu.
  3. Tap the Delete Account button.
  4. Confirm your request by providing your current password.

Data Erasure

Once completed, your account status is set to deleted, and all personal identifiers (including your names, email, phone number, and physical coordinates) are permanently cleared. This action cannot be undone.
