Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to stop using Zayda, you can permanently delete your account and remove your personal data at any time.

Important notice: Deleting your account is a permanent action. Your profile details, active sessions, saved addresses, favorites list, and shopping cart will be deleted immediately. Transaction records (e.g. invoice logs of past orders) will be securely archived and anonymized to comply with legal and financial reporting regulations.

How to delete your account:

1

Open the Zayda App

Launch the Zayda mobile application on your device and ensure you are logged in.

2

Go to Profile

Tap on the menu or navigation icon and select the Profile Settings option.

3

Tap Delete Account

Scroll down to the account controls section and tap the Delete Account button.

4

Confirm and Authorize

Enter your current password to authorize the request and tap to permanently delete your account.
