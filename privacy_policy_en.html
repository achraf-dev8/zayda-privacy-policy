Zayda Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 8 July 2026

Zayda operates the Zayda mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

We respect your privacy. We do NOT sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number

Location Information

  • GPS location (with your permission)
  • Selected delivery coordinates
  • Address details
  • Location details

Store Account Information

  • Store name
  • Store address
  • Store contact number
  • Product listings

Order Information

  • Orders placed
  • Delivery details
  • Order history

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide delivery and marketplace services
  • Show nearby stores
  • Calculate delivery distance and estimated time
  • Process and manage orders
  • Improve app functionality and performance

3. Location Usage

Location data is used strictly to:
  • Display nearby stores
  • Calculate ETA and delivery routes
  • Allow users to select their delivery address
Location access is optional but required for delivery features.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell personal information. We may share data:
  • With stores when you place an order
  • With technical service providers necessary to operate the platform
  • If required by law

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable security safeguards to protect your data. However, no digital system guarantees absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations.

7. Your Rights

You may:
  • Access or update your account information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request account deletion
To request deletion, contact us via email.

8. Children’s Privacy

Zayda does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years old.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
